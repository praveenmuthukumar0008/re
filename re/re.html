ABOUT US

Royal Enfield's journey began in Redditch, England, in 1901, with the creation of its first motorcycle. The brand's slogan, "Made like a gun, goes like a bullet" reflects its military roots, as the company initially produced weapons and ammunition before transitioning to motorcycles.

In the mid-20th century, Royal Enfield's popularity soared, particularly in India, where the Royal Enfield Bullet was introduced in 1949. By 1955, the Indian government ordered a large number of Bullets for its police and military forces, which led to the establishment of Enfield India. Eventually, the brand became wholly Indian-owned in 1994 when it was acquired by Eicher Motors.
